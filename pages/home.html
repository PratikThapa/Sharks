Info on Sharks

Sharks are a group of elasmobranch fish characterized by a cartilaginous skeleton, five to seven gill slits on the sides of the head, and pectoral fins that are not fused to the head. Modern sharks are classified within the clade Selachimorpha (or Selachii) and are the sister group to the Batoidea (rays and kin). Protection and Preservation of Sharks

Due to overfishing, habitat loss, and human activities, many species of sharks are becoming extinct. Different mass awareness activities, strict rules for hunting of sharks, and habitat protection must be done to preserve the shark species.
